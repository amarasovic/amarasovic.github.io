I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Computing, University of Utah. My primary research interests are at the confluence of **natural language processing (NLP)**, **multimodality**, and **explainable artificial intelligence (XAI)**, with a focus on building **trustworthy** and **intuitive language technology**.                  

I was a postdoc at the <a href="https://allenai.org/" style="text-decoration: none;">Allen Institute for AI</a> and <a href="https://www.cs.washington.edu/" style="text-decoration: none;">UW</a> working with <a href="https://nasmith.github.io/" style="text-decoration: none;">Noah A. Smith</a> and <a href="https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~yejin/" style="text-decoration: none;">Yejin Choi</a>. I did my PhD in the <a href="https://www.cl.uni-heidelberg.de/nlpgroup/" style="text-decoration: none;">Heidelberg University NLP Group</a>, where I was advised by <a href="https://www.cl.uni-heidelberg.de/~frank/" style="text-decoration: none;">Anette Frank</a>. Prior to receiving my PhD in Janurary 2019, I completed B.Sc. (2013) and M.Sc. (2015) in **Mathematics** at the University of Zagreb. I grew up in <a href="https://goo.gl/maps/Q8N6rZmWygHuiAzc9" style="text-decoration: none;">OmiÅ¡, Croatia</a>.
